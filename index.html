Self-Help & Signposting

Anxiety UK

Offers advice and support for people living with anxiety.

"Slow breathing is like an anchor in the midst of an emotional storm: the anchor won't make the storm go away, but it will hold you steady until it passes."

Russ Harris Good Housekeeping
Beat

Provides information and support for people affected by eating disorders.

"I think it’s really important to take the stigma away from mental health… My brain and my heart are really important to me. I don’t know why I wouldn’t seek help to have those things be as healthy as my teeth."

Kerry Washington from HuffPost
Campaign Against Living Miserably (CALM)

Supports anyone affected by suicide or suicidal thoughts.

"Just because no one else can heal or do your inner work for you doesn’t mean you can, should, or need to do it alone."

Lisa Olivera Good Housekeeping
FRANK

Provides confidential drugs advice and information.

"Self-care is how you take your power back."

Lalah Delia Good Housekeeping
Hearing Voices Network

Runs an online forum and local groups across the country.

"‘Positive vibes only’ isn’t a thing. Humans have a wide range of emotions and that’s OK."

Molly Bahr LMHC
Mind Out

Offers mental health advice and support for anyone who identifies as LGBTQ+.

"You, yourself, as much as anybody in the entire universe, deserve your love and affection."

Buddha Good Housekeeping
No Panic

Offers help and advice about anxiety disorders, including a helpline and recovery groups.

"This feeling will pass. The fear is real but the danger is not."

Cammie McGovern Good Housekeeping
Student Minds

Supports students with their mental health.

"There is no standard normal. Normal is subjective. There are seven billion versions of normal on this planet."

Matt Haig Reasons to Stay Alive
Young Minds

Provides confidential drugs advice and information.

"Sometimes the people around you won’t understand your journey. They don’t need to, it’s not for them."

Joubert Botha Good Housekeeping
